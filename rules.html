Server Rules

  • No Griefing at Spawn: Do not intentionally destroy or alter other players' builds at spawn without permission.
  • No Stealing: Do not take items or resources from other players' chests or areas without their consent.
  • Respect Claims: Respect land claims and protected areas established by other players. Do not build or mine within their territories without permission.
  • Be Courteous: Treat all players with respect and kindness. Avoid using bad language or excessive trash talk.
  • No Hacking or Cheating: Do not use any mods or hacks that give you an unfair advantage or compromise the server's integrity. Our admins and mods are watching your every move and will catch you if you're hacking. Offenders will be banned.
  • PvP Rules: If PvP (Player vs. Player) is allowed, only engage in combat with the consent of both parties. Do not spawn camp or harass players excessively.
  • No Spamming: Avoid excessive chat spamming or flooding the chat with messages. Use chat responsibly.
  • No Overpowered Mob Farms: Do not build mob farms that are overly powerful or cause excessive server lag.
  • No Inappropriate Builds: Do not build or create offensive, inappropriate, or obscene structures.
  • Looting and Exploring: When exploring, avoid looting villages and structures that other players may have plans for, unless given permission.
  • Keep the Environment Clean: Don't leave floating trees, creeper craters, or other eyesores in the world. Clean up after yourself.
  • Use Common Sense: Use your best judgment in all situations. If something feels wrong or questionable, ask a staff member or the server owner for guidance.
  • Reporting Issues: Report any issues, bugs, or rule violations to the server staff or owner promptly.
  • Have Fun: The primary goal is to have fun and enjoy the game together. Be a positive member of the community.

Note: Our server has admins and mods monitoring gameplay to ensure a fair and enjoyable experience. Any hacking will result in a ban. If you wish to appeal a ban, please contact DA FAT ELEPHANT.
